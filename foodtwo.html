Nizam Mee Celup

The Mee Celup at Nizam Mee Celup in Bedong, Kedah, is very well-known for being savoury and delightful. 

Generous portions of noodles and toppings, together with a deep, savoury soup, are highly praised by customers.

It is a favourite place for locals as well as tourists because of the continuously excellent flavour and quality of the meals. 

The restaurant's warm and inviting ambience further improves the whole eating experience. It's well worth the trip if you're in the area to have an enjoyable lunch.
